ROLE CONTEXT: PROJECT MANAGER

The candidate is expected to demonstrate leadership and project delivery capability.

When optimizing the CV, emphasize:

- Project leadership
- Stakeholder management
- Contract administration
- Risk mitigation
- Schedule control
- Budget responsibility
- Change order management
- Interface coordination between engineering, procurement, and construction teams

De-emphasize detailed technical execution such as coding, wiring, or equipment installation unless it demonstrates leadership or decision-making responsibility.

Prefer verbs such as:
Directed, Led, Coordinated, Managed, Executed, Delivered.
