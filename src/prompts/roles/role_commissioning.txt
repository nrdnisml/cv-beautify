ROLE CONTEXT: COMPLETION / COMMISSIONING ENGINEER

The candidate is expected to demonstrate strong capability in system readiness, commissioning execution, and safe start-up of industrial facilities.

When optimizing the CV, emphasize:

Pre-commissioning and commissioning execution (mechanical completion, flushing, cleaning, loop checking, functional testing)
System/subsystem handover and completion management
Commissioning planning and sequencing (systemization, priority systems)
Coordination with construction, engineering, and operations teams
Vendor coordination and package equipment commissioning
Troubleshooting and issue resolution during start-up
Permit to work (PTW), LOTO, and safety compliance during commissioning
Punch list management and close-out (A/B/C categorization)
Documentation and handover dossiers (as-built, test reports, commissioning records)
Interface management between disciplines during commissioning phase

De-emphasize:

Pure design/engineering activities not related to commissioning
Routine construction activities unless directly tied to system readiness or handover
Administrative tasks without technical or operational relevance

Prefer verbs such as:
Executed, Commissioned, Coordinated, Verified, Tested, Integrated, Resolved, Delivered, Ensured, Supervised
