INDUSTRY CONTEXT: GREEN / RENEWABLE ENERGY

Sector coverage:

Renewable energy generation (solar, wind, hydro, geothermal, biomass)
Energy transition & decarbonization projects
Sustainable fuels & bioenergy (including Sustainable Aviation Fuel – SAF)
Grid integration and energy storage systems

Typical project types include:

Solar power plants (PV, rooftop, utility-scale)
Wind farms (onshore & offshore)
Hydropower and geothermal plants
Battery Energy Storage Systems (BESS)
Green hydrogen production facilities
Waste-to-energy and biomass plants
Biofuel and Sustainable Aviation Fuel (SAF) production (e.g., avtur from palm oil waste / biomass feedstock)
EV charging infrastructure
Smart grid and grid modernization projects

Common project activities:

Feasibility study & resource assessment (including biomass feedstock evaluation)
FEED (Front End Engineering Design)
Detailed Engineering Design (DED)
EPC / EPCC (Engineering Procurement Construction Commissioning)
Process design for biofuel conversion (e.g., hydroprocessing, gasification)
Commissioning & performance testing
Operation & maintenance (O&M)
Asset optimization and lifecycle management

Relevant engineering systems:

Biomass processing and conversion systems
Biofuel refining units (hydrotreated vegetable oil – HVO, SAF processing)
Feedstock handling and pre-treatment systems
Process control systems (DCS, PLC, SCADA)
Power generation and utility systems
Energy storage systems (BESS)
Grid interconnection systems (substation, transformers)

Common standards and frameworks:

IEC / IEEE (electrical and power systems)
ISO (ISO 9001, ISO 14001, ISO 50001)
ASTM (e.g., ASTM D7566 for Sustainable Aviation Fuel)
IATA / ICAO guidelines (aviation fuel sustainability)
Environmental and sustainability regulations (ESG compliance, carbon reduction frameworks)

When rewriting CV descriptions:

Use terminology aligned with renewable energy, bioenergy, and sustainable fuel production.
Emphasize experience in biomass processing, fuel conversion, or energy systems where applicable.
Highlight involvement in EPC, commissioning, and process optimization.
Distinguish clearly between power generation projects and fuel production (SAF/biofuel).
Maintain strict accuracy; do not classify conventional refining or unrelated processing as sustainable fuel production without clear technical relevance.
